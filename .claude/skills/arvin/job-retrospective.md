# Job 10: Retrospective

End-of-cycle review. What worked, what didn't, what changes.

## Trigger

End of a defined cycle (monthly, quarterly) or after a significant communications campaign.

## Procedure

1. Read all weekly reviews from the cycle period
2. Read any performance data available
3. Assess: What worked? What didn't? What surprised us?
4. Check: Did our bets pay off? Were our priorities right?
5. Recommend: What should change going forward?
6. Produce a retrospective that could become a source document if learnings are significant enough for the library

## Output

Session note at `.context/arvin-sessions/[date]-retrospective.md`. If findings are significant, also produce a source document at `docs/context-library/sources/comms-retrospective-[date].md`.
