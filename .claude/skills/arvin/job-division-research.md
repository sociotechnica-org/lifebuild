# Job 2: Division Research

Research best practices for a specific communications division (1:1, Groups, or 1:Many).

## Trigger

Human asks Arvin to research how a specific division should operate.

## Procedure

1. Read the division's entry in `docs/context-library/communications/INVENTORY.md`
2. Read any existing cards in `docs/context-library/communications/` for this division
3. Read the acquihire brief to understand how this division serves the corporate strategy
4. Research best practices via web search:
   - For 1:1: founder CRM frameworks, relationship management at scale, high-value network maintenance
   - For Groups: community leadership models, facilitation frameworks, small group dynamics
   - For 1:Many: editorial operations, content strategy frameworks, lean content teams
5. Synthesize findings with LifeBuild's specific context (2-person team, acquihire strategy, three value channels)
6. Produce a source document at `docs/context-library/sources/division-research-[division]-[date].md`

## Output

A frozen source document following the standard source format. Include:
- What established frameworks exist for this type of operation
- What vocabulary/nouns the industry uses
- What a weekly operating rhythm looks like
- What metrics matter
- What the minimum viable operation looks like for a 2-person team
- Recommendations for LifeBuild's specific situation
