# Job 7: The Right 50

Build and maintain the O1 target list — the 50 people whose awareness of your work matters most for the acquihire strategy.

## Trigger

Human asks "Who are our right 50?" or when the master strategy is complete and the target list needs to be built. Also runs as a periodic refresh during Operate mode.

## Key Principle

From the acquihire brief: "This channel does not require a large audience. It requires the right fifty people to have seen the work."

From the Orbit Model: O1 = Advocates/Ambassadors — most engaged, highest gravity. Start here.

## Procedure

1. Read the acquihire brief — target acquirer shapes, what creates value
2. Read the master communications strategy
3. Read previous Right 50 list if one exists (`.context/arvin-sessions/right-50.md`)

4. **Build or refresh the list with the human:**

   **By acquirer shape:**
   - Notion-shaped (pre-IPO productivity) — who are the key product and engineering leaders?
   - Remote-first dev tools (Series B-D) — who are the engineering leads and CTOs?
   - AI Labs (Anthropic-tier) — who are the research and applied AI leads?
   - Large platform (Figma, Atlassian, GitLab) — who are the product visionaries?

   **By role type:**
   - Engineering leadership at target companies
   - Product executives at target companies
   - VCs/GPs with future-of-work thesis
   - Founders/builders in adjacent spaces (thought leadership network)
   - Developer community leaders (open source traction multipliers)

   **For each person, capture:**
   - Name, role, company
   - Why they matter (which value channel they serve)
   - Current relationship status: Unknown / Aware / Engaged / Champion
   - Last meaningful interaction (if any)
   - Next action: what would move them one level deeper?

5. **Assess coverage:**
   - How many of the 50 have we reached?
   - Which acquirer shapes are underrepresented?
   - Where are the biggest gaps?
   - What's the highest-leverage next action?

6. **Recommend focus:**
   - Top 5 relationships to invest in this month
   - What content would reach the people we haven't reached?
   - Any conferences, communities, or channels where multiple targets congregate?

## Output

Working document at `.context/arvin-sessions/right-50.md` (updated in place, not a new file each time). This is a living list, not a frozen source document.
