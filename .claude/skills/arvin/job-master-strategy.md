# Job 1: Master Strategy

Draft the overarching communications strategy that frames all division and channel work. This is the first Build mode job — everything else references it.

## Trigger

Human asks Arvin to create or revise the master communications strategy.

## Procedure

1. Read the acquihire brief (corporate strategy)
2. Read the socialization factory vision (operations vision)
3. Read the communications operations research (frameworks)
4. Read the communications inventory (what needs to be built)
5. Read any existing communications cards

6. Draft the master strategy covering:

   **Strategic positioning:**
   - Who are we? What do we stand for? What's our voice?
   - Apply byline authority principle: Danvers and Jess post as themselves, not as "LifeBuild"
   - What is our position-based authority? (37signals model: pick a position, publish from it)

   **Content pillars (3-5 themes):**
   - What are the repeating themes that anchor all content?
   - Each pillar should serve at least one acquihire value channel
   - Candidate pillars from research: factory model, context library, human-AI teaming, building in public

   **PESO weighting:**
   - For acquihire: Owned > Shared > Earned > Paid
   - What owned assets do we need? (blog, demo video, open source docs)
   - What shared channels? (LinkedIn first — channel-market fit for acquihire audience)

   **Three divisions: how they serve the acquihire strategy:**
   - 1:1: Who are the right 50? How does relationship management feed the acquihire?
   - Groups: How does community leadership build reputation and credibility?
   - 1:Many: How does content production create thought leadership visibility?

   **Three value channels: mapping communications to acquihire outcomes:**
   - Thought leadership → right 50 people see the work
   - Open source traction → context library gets stars and usage
   - Internal champions → target company employees encounter and use the work

   **Resource allocation:**
   - Where to invest a 2-person team's limited attention
   - One channel first, then expand (channel-founder fit assessment)
   - What to say no to

   **Sequencing:**
   - What to build first, what to defer
   - Apply "strategy before factory" principle

   **Success signals:**
   - How do we know it's working? (per acquihire brief: right user retention, unprompted advocacy, target company employee usage, demo video circulation)

   **Operating model:**
   - Content backlog (pull system) not rigid calendar (push system)
   - Appetite + circuit breaker for content production
   - Weekly review cadence (15 min)
   - Betting table for content decisions

## Output

A source document at `docs/context-library/sources/master-communications-strategy-[date].md`. This is the highest-priority source document — everything else references it.
