# Job 4: Channel Strategy

Research channel-specific strategy for a communications medium (LinkedIn, newsletter, blog, etc.).

## Trigger

Human asks Arvin to develop strategy for a specific channel.

## Procedure

1. Read the master communications strategy and content pillars
2. Read the acquihire brief — which value channels does this medium serve?
3. Read the research document — relevant channel and framework sections
4. Read any existing channel/division cards

5. **Assess channel fit before recommending adoption:**

   **Channel-market fit:** Is this where the right 50 people actually are? Where do engineering leaders and product executives at target companies spend time?

   **Channel-founder fit:** Can Danvers or Jess sustain this format? Text, video, audio — what's natural? What won't feel like a chore at week 8?

   **One channel first principle:** If this isn't the first channel, has the first channel been mastered? Don't add a second channel until the first is producing consistent, quality output.

6. If fit is confirmed, research best practices:
   - How do small teams use this channel effectively?
   - What content types perform? What cadence works?
   - How do acquihire-stage startups use this channel for targeted visibility?
   - What does build-in-public look like on this channel?

7. Synthesize with LifeBuild's specific positioning, pillars, and audience
8. Produce a source document with channel strategy recommendations

## Output

A source document at `docs/context-library/sources/channel-strategy-[channel]-[date].md`.
